Privacy Policy

Effective: 20 August 2025
RelNet Operations Private Ltd

Summary: We collect the minimum data necessary to operate our website and services, and we do not sell your personal data. Your privacy is a priority — we handle all information with care and transparency.

[ 01 ]

Information We Collect

  • Contact Data: name, email address, phone number, and message content when you contact us.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device information, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: used to operate and improve the Site; you can control cookies via your browser settings.
[ 02 ]

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide our Services.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site, including analytics.
  • To send service and policy notices (with consent where required).
  • To protect against, investigate, and deter fraudulent or illegal activity.
[ 03 ]

Legal Bases

Where applicable, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases: consent, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests in operating and improving our services.

[ 04 ]

Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: trusted vendors who assist in operations (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests from authorities.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with appropriate safeguards.
[ 05 ]

International Transfers

We may transfer data to countries outside your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable laws to ensure your data remains protected.

[ 06 ]

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by applicable law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

[ 07 ]

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data held by us.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority where applicable.
[ 08 ]

Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain Site features. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising or sell cookie data to third parties.

[ 09 ]

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

[ 10 ]

Children's Privacy

Our Site and Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us to request deletion.

[ 11 ]

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above reflects the latest revision. Your continued use of the Site after changes become effective signifies your acceptance of the updated Policy.

[ 12 ]
